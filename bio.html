BIO

Andrew Welling (born January 2002) is an LGBTQIA+ composer studying and working in Virginia, USA. He has participated in numerous collaborations with musicians and artists and his music explores identity, acoustics, and perception. He is heavily inspired by artistic, philosophical, and scientific concepts.

"I am passionate about the exploration and application of different microtonal techniques and the investigation of intersections between the arts and sciences. I believe in the proliferation of new music through collaborative, constructive, and inclusive interactions. My music is shaped by my life experiences as a queer composer, which are articulated through an ever evolving artistic lens. As I continue to grow as an artist and human being, I hope that by sharing my ideas I can make a meaningful impact on someone somewhere in the world."
