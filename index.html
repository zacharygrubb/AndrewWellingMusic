PREMIERE: OFFAL, for solo violin

Offal was commissioned by the Impulse New Music Festival and was premiered by Ambroise Aubrun on September 4, 2022.

This piece seeks to capture a sense of the spontaneity of improvisation through a meticulously precise architecture. It communicates directly and in a way which is accessible and relevant.
