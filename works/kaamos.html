KAAMOS

Kaamos, as a piece, exists as a theoretical construction. Written in the Winter of 2021 as a reflection on the pandemic, it captures feelings of isolation, frustration, and mourning. This primarily considers the horizontal in an equal tempered sense - approximating the upper partials of overtone series, and the vertical in a just-intonation sense - where chord tones are perfectly in tune with each other according to their ratio relationships. It exists in several versions, arranged and altered for different instrumental ensembles. So far, 3 versions exist: saxophone quartet, electronic media, and string quartet.
