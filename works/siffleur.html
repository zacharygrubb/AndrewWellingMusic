siffleur

Siffleur is a brief composition for string ensemble which explores microtonality in a familiar context. The very simple melody and round evokes the lapping of waves and conveys a sort of meditative introspection. The application of microtonality in this piece is inspired by the concept of ‘macrotonality’ championed by British composer Julian Anderson.

Andrew Welling · Siffleur (MIDI realization) (2021)
